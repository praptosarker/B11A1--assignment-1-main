The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in
renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Dear friends,Today, we dream of a New Bangladesh 2.0—an inclusive, innovative, and sustainable nation. This vision is guided by three goals: zero poverty, zero unemployment, and zero net carbon emissions. Bangladesh has always been a land of resilience and creativity. Now, we must harness the power of technology and entrepreneurship to build a future where no one is left behind. Let us empower our youth, embrace social business, and ensure equality, with women and men leading together. This is our chance to show the world that progress doesn’t have to come at the cost of humanity or the planet. Together, we will create a Bangladesh that inspires hope, justice, and opportunity for all. Thank you.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
